The ubiquitous presence of devices and the rapid change of our environment in the last decades made many parts of our life easier, but it also changed our bodies. People are sitting in chairs or car seats most of the day, or do very repetitive and specialized movements throughout the day.
This lack of movement variety and poor alignment often leads to wear and tear, just like driving a car without proper alignment for 100,000 miles. In this case, we would need to deal with many broken tires. So, suddenly people need body tune-ups, in severe cases with surgery, in most cases they attempt to overcome the defects in the gym or exercise classes. Here, they learn new repetitive movements that can possibly cause new problems.
Do you think that it is time to change the way you move and to learn how your own body works? The exercise program {{config.TITLE}} brings you there. It consists of twelve easy-to understand video exercises, that come with a balanced mix of information and instructions. A key concept is to overcome imprinted patterns through the use of visual imagery. Changing these patterns will make you move beyond exercise. Instead of going to the gym or exercising in small chunks per week, you can integrate our movement suggestions in your daily life.
